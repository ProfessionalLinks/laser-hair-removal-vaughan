What to Expect During the Treatment

The laser hair removal process involves using a handheld device that emits concentrated light beams. During the treatment, clients may experience a slight tingling or snapping sensation, but most find the procedure to be relatively painless. Each session typically lasts between 15 to 60 minutes, depending on the size of the area being treated. Multiple sessions are required to achieve the desired results, as hair grows in different cycles and the laser targets follicles in the active growth phase.

Post-Treatment Care and Results

After undergoing laser hair removal Vaughan, it's essential to follow post-treatment care instructions to ensure the best outcomes. This includes avoiding direct sun exposure, applying soothing creams, and refraining from using harsh skin products on the treated area. Results can vary, but most clients notice a significant reduction in hair growth after a few sessions. With proper care and maintenance, the effects of laser hair removal can be long-lasting, providing a smooth and hair-free appearance.
